    Tricks Index¶

    This index lists every complete trick and routine taught across Giobbi's published works. Each entry gives: the effect type, the primary technique(s) required, and a source reference by chapter. Technique articles in this wiki are linked where relevant.

    Effect type abbreviations: - LOC — Location / discovery of a chosen card - PRED — Prediction - TRANS — Transposition (cards exchange places) - ASSEM — Assembly (cards gather, e.g., aces come together) - PROD — Production / appearance of a card - VAN — Vanish - CHANGE — Transformation / color change - MENT — Mental / ESP effect - GAMB — Gambling demonstration - COINC — Coincidence - AMB — Ambitious Card (card keeps returning to top) - MISC — Other / compound plot

    Genii Magazine: "The Ten Best Card Tricks"¶

    Giobbi's personal list of the ten strongest and most artistic card tricks, with notes on his preferred versions. This article is a critical essay as much as a performance guide.

    Trick Notes
    Brainwave Dai Vernon's version, with Paul Fox's red/blue back idea — "arguably the best mental card trick"
    (8 further selections) The article discusses effects Giobbi considers the pinnacle of card magic; several are attributed to Juan Tamariz and other masters

    Unexpected Agenda¶

    A 2024 daily-reader format book (365 entries organized by month). Rather than individual tricks, entries contain reflections, tips, presentations, and complete ideas. Notable entries include:

    • The Zodiac Clock — A presentation adaptation of the Clock Trick using zodiac signs
    • An Opener for Table Magic (February 15) — A short complete effect
    • Angel's Peek — My Handling — A technique-and-presentation entry

    (The Agenda series books are better treated as a daily study supplement than a tricks reference.)

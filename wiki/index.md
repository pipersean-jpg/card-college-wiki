# Card Magic Wiki
### Based on Roberto Giobbi's *Card College* series and related works

This wiki covers the techniques, principles, and theory of sleight-of-hand card magic as taught by Roberto Giobbi across his complete body of work. Sources cited throughout are:

- **CC1–CC5**: *Card College*, Volumes 1–5 (the main five-volume course)
- **CCL / CCLR**: *Card College Light* and *Card College Lighter* (condensed introductions)
- **HA**: *Hidden Agenda* · **SA**: *Secret Agenda* · **SS**: *Sharing Secrets*

## 3. False Shuffles

## 13. Originators

*Profiles of the magicians and thinkers whose work Giobbi draws on throughout the series.*

- [Dai Vernon](originators/dai-vernon.md) — "The Professor"; techniques attributed: Convincing Control, Vernon Multiple Shift, Pull-Through Shuffle variant, Vernon Replacement, Cold-Deck Cut; philosophy: "Be Natural"
- [Arturo de Ascanio](originators/arturo-de-ascanio.md) — Spanish grandmaster; the Ascanio Spread and display philosophy; his personal friendship with Giobbi; died April 6, 1997 with cards in hand
- [Juan Tamariz](originators/juan-tamariz.md) — Foreword writer for CC1; creator of T.N.T.; his concept of the "dynamic use of the gaze"; reference to *Sonata*
- [S.W. Erdnase](originators/sw-erdnase.md) — Pseudonymous author of *The Expert at the Card Table* (1902); identity never established; foundational vocabulary for card technique and gambling methods
- [Edward Marlo](originators/edward-marlo.md) — The most prolific creator of card sleights in history; Tilt, the Olram Subtlety, Olram's Jinx Switch; the "Olram = Marlo reversed" convention; Marlo vs. Vernon as a philosophical debate
- [Other Creators](originators/other-creators.md) — ~20 individual profiles: Robert-Houdin, Braue, Elmsley, Hamman, Zarrow, Houdini, Cardini, Carney, LePaul, Slydini, Fred Kaps, Balducci, Jordan, Veeser, Paul Curry, Henry Christ, and others
